Design Principles

We have created these design principles to help guide our design thinking and decision making

1. The design will consider funding as an end to end service

2. Recognise applicants and recipients as primary users and enable them to utilise available funds frictionlessly

3. Provide the department and wider government with confidence that money is being spent responsibly

4. Design a sustainable service that enables teams across departments to support current and future funding

5. Enable a consistent user experience across funds, through shared tooling and design standards

6. Design to enable the needs of all funds and recognise that one size doesn't fit all

7. The service must be scalable to respond to changing requirements
